Applicable demographic information that will help to explain the selection of eateries on this map:

  • 20-something
  • Employed full-time
  • Asian descent
  • Will pay for quality, but rarely over $30
  • Otherwise, cheap eats are preferred almost always
  • Partial to ethnic eateries

Color scheme:

  • Green: Cheap (<= $10)
  • Yellow: Medium ($10 < $ <= $25)
  • Red: Expensive (> $25)

Within the color scheme the restaurants are generally ordered by preference. I'm also intentionally omitting most common food chains, which is why Red Robin is not on this list. But I've got love for Red Robin.
